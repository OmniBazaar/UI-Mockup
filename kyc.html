KYC/AML Compliance

Important: OmniBazaar is a decentralized platform. KYC requirements may vary based on your jurisdiction and transaction volume.

When KYC May Be Required

  • High-value transactions (typically over $10,000 USD equivalent)
  • Frequent trading activity that triggers regulatory thresholds
  • Certain jurisdictions with specific requirements
  • Professional or business accounts

KYC Process

When required, KYC is handled by third-party providers who specialize in regulatory compliance:

  • Identity document verification
  • Address verification
  • Source of funds verification (for high amounts)
  • Ongoing monitoring for suspicious activity

Privacy Protection

We prioritize your privacy while meeting regulatory requirements:

  • Minimal data collection
  • Third-party KYC providers with strict privacy policies
  • Data encryption and secure storage
  • Right to data deletion when legally permitted
