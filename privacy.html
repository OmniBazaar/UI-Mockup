Privacy-First Decentralized Architecture

1. Privacy Overview

OmniBazaar operates as a decentralized marketplace protocol that minimizes data collection and maximizes user control over personal information:

  • Decentralized by Design: Most data is stored on your device or distributed networks
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect what's necessary for core functionality
  • User Control: You decide what information to share and with whom
  • Transparent Processing: All data handling is governed by smart contracts and open protocols

Key Principle: Your data belongs to you. We provide tools and infrastructure, but you maintain control over your information.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Data Type Storage Location Purpose
Profile Information IPFS/Local Display name, bio, avatar for marketplace identity
Listing Data IPFS/Blockchain Product descriptions, images, pricing
Messages Encrypted P2P Communication between buyers and sellers
Wallet Addresses Blockchain Transaction processing and payment

Automatically Collected Information

  • Transaction Data: Recorded on public blockchains
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized interaction data (opt-in)
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information (temporary)
  • Performance Data: Error logs and performance metrics

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification documents (handled by third-party KYC providers if required)
  • Credit card or bank account information
  • Social security numbers or government IDs
  • Detailed browsing history or tracking across other websites
  • Private keys or seed phrases (always remain with you)

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information only for legitimate purposes related to operating the marketplace:

Core Platform Functions

  • Facilitating transactions between buyers and sellers
  • Displaying listings and user profiles
  • Processing payments through smart contracts
  • Providing customer support and dispute resolution
  • Maintaining platform security and preventing fraud

Platform Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve user experience (anonymized)
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Developing new features based on user needs
  • Optimizing platform performance

Legal and Safety

  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations
  • Preventing fraudulent or illegal activity
  • Protecting the rights and safety of users
  • Responding to legal process when required

4. Data Storage & IPFS

Distributed Storage Architecture

OmniBazaar uses multiple storage solutions to maximize privacy and data ownership:

IPFS (InterPlanetary File System): Your listings, images, and profile data are stored on the distributed IPFS network, which you control through your private keys.

Storage Type Data Stored Control Privacy Level
Local Device Settings, preferences, draft content Full User Control Complete Privacy
IPFS Network Listings, images, public profile data User-Controlled Keys Pseudonymous
Blockchain Transactions, smart contract interactions Immutable & Transparent Pseudonymous
Encrypted P2P Private messages, communication End-to-End Encrypted Private

Data Retention

  • IPFS Data: Persists as long as nodes continue to pin it
  • Blockchain Data: Permanently recorded and cannot be deleted
  • Local Data: Under your complete control
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for up to 2 years

5. Information Sharing

When We Share Information

Due to our decentralized architecture, information sharing is limited and controlled:

  • Public Listings: Voluntarily shared on IPFS for marketplace visibility
  • Transaction Parties: Necessary details shared with buyers/sellers for transactions
  • Service Providers: Limited data shared with infrastructure providers (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to prevent illegal activity
  • Community Moderation: Dispute-related information shared with arbitrators

Information We Don't Share

  • Private messages between users
  • Wallet private keys or seed phrases
  • Personal contact information without consent
  • Detailed browsing or usage patterns
  • Any information for advertising or marketing purposes

6. Cookies and Tracking

OmniBazaar uses minimal tracking technologies:

Essential Cookies

  • Session management and authentication
  • Shopping cart and transaction state
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • User preferences and settings

Optional Analytics

  • Anonymous usage statistics (opt-in only)
  • Performance monitoring (error tracking)
  • Feature usage analysis (to improve UX)

Your Control: You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings or through our privacy preferences.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Data Control Rights

  • Access: View all data associated with your account
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Remove data from systems under our control
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats
  • Consent Withdrawal: Opt out of optional data collection

Blockchain Data Considerations

Important Note: Data recorded on public blockchains cannot be deleted or modified. This includes transaction records and some smart contract interactions. Consider this before engaging in blockchain transactions.

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Use the privacy controls in your account settings
  • Contact our privacy team through the contact form
  • Submit requests through our decentralized governance system
  • Manage IPFS content through your wallet interface

9. Data Security

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All sensitive data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations and authentication
  • Smart Contract Security: Audited and verified contract code
  • Infrastructure Security: Secure hosting and network protection
  • Regular Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection

User Security Responsibilities

  • Secure your wallet and private keys
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep software and browsers updated
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Provide clear information about the incident
  • Offer guidance on protective measures
  • Work with law enforcement as appropriate
  • Implement additional safeguards to prevent recurrence

10. Children's Privacy

OmniBazaar is designed for users 18 years and older:

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18
  • Users must verify they are of legal age to enter contracts
  • Parents who discover their child has provided information should contact us immediately
  • We will delete any information from minors upon discovery

Cryptocurrency Considerations: Trading cryptocurrency and digital assets involves financial risk and requires adult responsibility and legal capacity.
