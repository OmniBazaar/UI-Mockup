Safety & Security

Learn how to stay safe while trading on OmniBazaar

🔒 Security Features

Smart Contract Escrow

All transactions are protected by audited smart contracts that hold funds until both parties confirm completion.

Decentralized Identity

Optional KYC verification through decentralized oracles enhances trust without compromising privacy.

Reputation System

Blockchain-based reputation scores help identify trustworthy traders based on transaction history.

Dispute Resolution

Community-driven arbitration system fairly resolves conflicts with transparent voting.

⚠️ Safety Tips for Buyers

  • Always verify seller reputation and reviews before purchasing
  • Use escrow for all transactions - never send payment directly
  • Check product descriptions and photos carefully
  • Communicate only through the platform's messaging system
  • Report suspicious listings or behavior immediately
  • Don't share personal information or payment details outside the platform
  • Verify tracking information for physical goods
  • Test digital goods immediately upon receipt

💼 Safety Tips for Sellers

  • Provide accurate descriptions and clear photos
  • Ship items promptly and provide tracking information
  • Package items securely to prevent damage
  • Keep proof of shipment and delivery
  • Respond to buyer questions promptly and honestly
  • Never ask buyers to complete transactions off-platform
  • Report problematic buyers to help protect the community

🚨 Recognizing Scams

Common Red Flags:

  • Requests to complete transactions outside OmniBazaar
  • Prices that seem too good to be true
  • Pressure to complete transactions quickly
  • Requests for additional payments after purchase
  • Sellers with no history or suspicious reviews
  • Requests for personal information or passwords
