Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a listing on OmniBazaar?
To create a listing, click "Create Listing" from the main page or your dashboard. Fill in the required information including title, description, price, and upload high-quality images. Your listing will be published to the blockchain network once you confirm the transaction.
What payment methods are accepted?
OmniBazaar primarily operates with cryptocurrency payments including XOM (our native token), Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other supported cryptocurrencies. We also support traditional payment methods in some regions through our payment partners.
How does the escrow system work?
Our smart contract-based escrow system holds funds securely until both parties confirm the transaction is complete. When you make a purchase, funds are locked in escrow. They're released to the seller only when you confirm receipt of the item or after a predetermined time period.
What if I have a dispute with a seller?
If you have an issue with a transaction, you can open a dispute through your transaction history. Our decentralized dispute resolution system uses community arbitrators and smart contracts to resolve conflicts fairly and transparently.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our support team through the contact form, community forums, or live chat. As a decentralized platform, we also encourage community support through our Discord and Telegram channels.
