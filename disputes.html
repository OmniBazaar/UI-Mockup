Dispute Resolution

Fair, transparent, and community-driven dispute resolution for all marketplace transactions

How Dispute Resolution Works

1

File a Dispute

If there's an issue with your transaction, file a dispute within 30 days of the transaction.

2

Evidence Submission

Both parties submit evidence including photos, messages, and transaction details.

3

Community Arbitration

Neutral arbitrators from the community review the evidence and make a decision.

4

Automatic Resolution

Smart contracts automatically execute the arbitrators' decision and release funds accordingly.
