Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn referral rewards? ▼
You earn commission on all trading fees generated by your referrals. The more active your referrals are, the more you earn. Commissions are paid out in XOM tokens weekly.
When do referrals become active? ▼
A referral becomes active after they complete KYC verification and make their first transaction on the platform. Pending referrals have 30 days to activate their account.
How long do I earn from my referrals? ▼
You earn lifetime commissions from all your referrals as long as they remain active on the platform. There's no expiration on referral relationships.
Can I track my referrals' activity? ▼
Yes, you can see aggregated statistics about your referrals' activity, including transaction counts and your earnings. Individual transaction details are kept private for user privacy.
