What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. OmniBazaar uses minimal cookies to provide essential functionality.

How We Use Cookies

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Authentication and security Session
Preferences User settings and language 1 year
Analytics Anonymous usage statistics (opt-in) 30 days

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.
